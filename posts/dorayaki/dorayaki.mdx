<StyledParagraph>Honey flavored pancakes filled with red bean paste, is one of our go to desserts. Fluffy and moist pancakes are the perfect vessel for any sweet filling.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 20 min | 15 min |   8-10 dorayaki  | Intermediate  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>4 large eggs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>⅔ cup sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp honey</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 ⅓ cup all-purpose flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1-2 Tbsp water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1lb red bean paste</ListItem>

## Steps

1. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar and honey until the mixture becomes pale and fluffy (5min)
2. Sift flour and baking powder into egg mixture and whisk until well incorporated
3. Let batter rest for at least 15min and add water if consistency is too thick
4. Heat a non stick pan on med heat and oil the pan with a paper towel.
5. Ladle approximately 3tbsp of batter into the pan and flip after 1 min once the pancakes are golden brown. Cook for another 1 min on the other side
6. Spread red bean filling into 2 pancakes and enjoy!
