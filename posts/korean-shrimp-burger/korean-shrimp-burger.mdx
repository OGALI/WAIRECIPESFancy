# Ingredients

## Burger patty
- <ListItem>8 large minced shrimp</ListItem>
- <ListItem>8 large diced shrimp</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tbsp all purpose flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp water</ListItem>

## Breading
- <ListItem>1/4 cup all purpose flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 large egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup panko bread crumbs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>pinch of salt and pepper</ListItem>

## Garnish
- <ListItem>2 toasted with butter brioche burger buns</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup chopped lettuce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Red onion</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Burger sauce</ListItem>

## Burger sauce
- <ListItem>4 tbsp mayonnaise</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp yellow mustard</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp wasabi</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp chopped onion</ListItem>
- <ListItem>light pinch of pepper</ListItem>

# Steps

1. Take around 8 shrimp and mince them with a knife or blend in a food processor until it forms a chunky paste.
2. Chop 8 other shrimp into larger chunks and mix minced shrimp and chunky shrimp with flour and water.
3. Form into a burger patties (3-4)
4. Put flour, egg and panko in separate bowls.
5. Coat burger patty with flour first, then egg and finally coat in panko
6. Fry in an pain with oil on med-high heat until crispy (~ 10 minutes)
