<StyledParagraph>Starting off our summer edition with these delicious protein pancakes using vanilla protein powder. These pancakes turned out light and fluffy with such a good banana and vanilla flavour.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 min | 15 min |  2  | Easy  |

## Ingredients
- <ListItem>1/2-1 banana</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup oats</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 scoop protein powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 teaspoon baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tablespoons plant based milk (add more if needed)</ListItem>

## Steps 
1. Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth 
2. Cook pancakes on med-high heat in a pan. To make dollar pancakes, spoon 1 heaping tbsp of batter into the pan and flip after 2-3 min
3. Serve with toppings of your choice (fruit of choice, yogurt, maple syrup, cinnamon)
