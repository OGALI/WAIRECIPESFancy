# Ingredients

## Bread 
- <ListItem>80g water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>80g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>68g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>8g yeast</ListItem>
- <ListItem>7g salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>12g dried milk powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g egg (2 large eggs)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20g heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>400g bread flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g unsalted butter</ListItem>
## Matcha Filling 
- <ListItem>105g unsalted butter, room temp</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g caster sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>50g AP flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g milk Power</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>5g matcha</ListItem>

# Steps 

1. To activate the yeast, mix water, milk and sugar in a large bowl. Heat in the microwave until warm. Add the yeast and let sit for 5min.
2. Add salt, dried milk powder, egg and cream to the yeast mixture and stir 
3. Add the bread flour and knead by hand or in a stand mixer with a bread attachment for 15 minutes. Once the mixture starts to come together, fold in the butter
4. Once the dough is smooth, place in a bowl and let proof until doubled in size (2-3h)
5. Measure the dough out into 80g pieces and let rest for 15min 
6. To make the filing, cream the butter with the sugar until fluffy. Stir in the eggs. Sift in the flour, milk powder and matcha. Stir until well incororated and mixture is smooth
7. Roll a piece of dough into a rectangle (15-20cm), spread filling over the dough like you're buttering a toast 
8. Roll up the dough along the longer side. Split dough with a knife through the center and braid. Once braided form a circle to close off the bun 
9. Proof buns for another 45-60 min until 1.5x the size 
10. Brush milk over the buns and bake for 10-15 min at 375 deg F
