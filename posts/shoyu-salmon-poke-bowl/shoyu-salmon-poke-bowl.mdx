<StyledParagraph>This is a traditional Hawaiian dish, consisting of cubes of raw fish marinated with soy sauce. The most important thing: get sashimi-grade salmon 🍣!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 10 min | N/A |  2  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>6 ounces fresh salmon filet</ListItem>
- <ListItem>¼ cup premium soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp brown sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>¼ white onion cut into stips</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp sesame oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp green onions finely chopped</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp sesame seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp seaweed</ListItem>

## Steps

1. Cut the salmon into large chunks or cubes
2. Mix in the rest of the ingredients with the salmon  
3. Serve over a bed a rice 🍚 and that's all!
