<StyledParagraph>Two peas in a pod, these buns go hand in hand. These are fluffy and delicious milk breads topped with a crunchy and sweet coffee/matcha topping. Definitely try this one out and let us know how it goes!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hours | 10-12min |  13-14 buns  | Difficult  |

# Ingredients
## Bread 
- <ListItem>80g water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>80g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>68g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>8g yeast</ListItem>
- <ListItem>7g salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>12g dried milk powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g egg (2 large eggs)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20g heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>400g bread flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g unsalted butter</ListItem>
## Coffee topping
- <ListItem>10g warm water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>6g instant coffee powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g unsalted butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>50g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g flour</ListItem>
## Matcha topping
-<ListItem>60g unsalted butter</ListItem>
-<ListItem>1 egg</ListItem>
-<ListItem>50g powdered sugar</ListItem>
-<ListItem>50g flour</ListItem>    
-<ListItem>6g matcha</ListItem>
-<ListItem>8g milk</ListItem> 

## Steps

1. To activate the yeast, mix water, milk and sugar in a large bowl. Heat in the microwave until warm. Add the yeast and let sit for 5min.
2. Add salt, dried milk powder, egg and cream to the yeast mixture and stir 
3. Add the bread flour and knead by hand or in a stand mixer with a bread attachment for 15 minutes. Once the mixture starts to come together, fold in the butter
4. Once the dough is smooth, place in a bowl and let proof until doubled in size (2-3h)
5. Measure the dough out into 60g pieces and let rest for 15min 
6. Form circular shapped balls and let proof for another hour 
7. To make the coffee filling, dissolve coffee in warm water. In a seperate bowl whisk butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add coffee mixture and stir
8. Add 1 egg to mixture and whisk until well incorporated. Finally, stir in flour and mix until smooth 
9. For the matcha filling, whisk butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg, milk and stir 
10. Sift flour and matcha into egg mixture and stir until smooth 
11. Place each filling in a pipping bag and pipe in a circular motion until the bread top is completely covered
12. Bake in the oven at 355 deg F for 10-12 min
