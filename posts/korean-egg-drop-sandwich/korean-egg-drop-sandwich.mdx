<StyledParagraph> Twist on the popular street food sandwich and sandwich chains in Korea. Simple and delicious dish to enjoy anytime of the day.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 10 min | 5 min |  1  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>2 slices of white bread</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Lettuce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Crab meat or mock crab meat</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 scrambled eggs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp Japanese mayo</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp Ketchup</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Cheese</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Ground seaweed</ListItem>

## Steps 
1. Scramble eggs and cook cabbage separately
2. Toast bread in butter in a pan on medium heat 
3. Spread ketchup and mayo on the bread and assemble the rest of the sandwich ingredients
4. Cook in pan for a few more minutes until golden brown and until cheese is melted
