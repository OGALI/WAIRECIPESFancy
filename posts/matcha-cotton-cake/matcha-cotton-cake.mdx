## Ingredients

- <ListItem>6 eggs, seperated</ListItem>
- <ListItem>90g AP flour</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>10g matcha</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100ml milk</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>120g granulated sugar</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Stir the milk and oil together in a large bowl. Heat on a stovetop or microwave until warm
2. Sift the flour and matcha over the warm mixture and whisk until smooth. Add the egg yolks and whisk until incorporated
3. In a seperate bowl, whisk the egg whites until frothy and poor in the sugar gradually while whisking. Continue until stiff peaks 
4. Fold the egg whites into the bowl with the matcha batter in 2 additions 
5. Poor batter into a loaf pan (8.5x4.5 inch) or a square pan (7x7 inch)
6. Place pan in a water bath with boiling water until the water reaches ~ half way of the pan
7. Bake in the oven at 300 deg F for 60-70 min 
8. Let the cake cool before slicing and serving ☜(ﾟヮﾟ☜)
