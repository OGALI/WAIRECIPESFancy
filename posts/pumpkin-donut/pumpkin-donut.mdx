<StyledParagraph>Super healthy and mouth watering baked donuts with a hint of pumpkin spice.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 15 min | 20 min |  12 donuts  | Easy  |

# Ingredients

## Batter
- <ListItem>1/2 cup pumpkin puree</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup brown sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp coconut oil (melted), plus extra for greasing</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp apple sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3/4 cup gluten-free oat flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 teaspoon ground ginger</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/8 teaspoon ground cloves</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/8 teaspoon fine sea salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 teaspoon baking soda</ListItem>

## Glaze
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp powdered sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 Tbsp plant based milk</ListItem>

## Caramel
- <ListItem>1/4 cup sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp coconut milk</ListItem>

# Steps 
1. Mix dry and wet ingredients(pumpkin, brown sugar, oil, apple sauce, vanilla, vinegar) separately and then combine. 
2. Pipe into donut baking molds. Bake at 350F for 18-20 mins.
3. For the glaze, mix ingredients together
4. For the caramel, heat the sugar in sauce pan until golden brown on medium heat. Turn off heat and add milk
