## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1/2 cup quick oats or rolled oats</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 cup of plant based milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 scoop of your fave protein</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp chia seeds</ListItem>

## Toppings

- <ListItem>Yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Banana</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Raspberry</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Blueberry</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Hemp seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Chia seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>All bran cereal</ListItem>

## Steps 
1. Mix oats, milk, protein powder, and chia seeds. Leave in the fridge overnight or for at least 1 hour.
2. Top with your fave fruits, nuts or nut butter.
