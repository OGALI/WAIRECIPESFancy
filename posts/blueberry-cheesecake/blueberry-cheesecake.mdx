<StyledParagraph>Simple and easy no bake cheesecake that will satisfy any dessert craving. If you're looking for that guilt free, but decadent dessert fix, here's your answer!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hour | 5 min |  6  | Intermediate |

# Ingredients

## Crust
- <ListItem>1/2 cup raw almonds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup medjool dates</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp rolled oats</ListItem>

## Cheesecake filling
-<ListItem>1/2 cup cashews soaked overnight</ListItem>
-<ListItem>160g silken tofu</ListItem>
-<ListItem>1/4 cup coconut milk</ListItem>
-<ListItem>3 Tbsp maple syrup</ListItem>
-<ListItem>1 tsp vanilla extract</ListItem>
-<ListItem>1 Tbsp lemon juice</ListItem>
-<ListItem>1/2 lemon zested</ListItem>

## Blueberry topping
- <ListItem>1 cup blueberries</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp lemon juice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp maple syrup</ListItem>

# Steps
1. For the crust: In a blender, grind almonds until coarse and then add in rest of ingredients until it comes together. Press pie crust into a 6 inch pan
2. For the filling: Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Poor mixture over crust and refrigerate for at least 5h.
3. For the jam: Cook all ingredients down in a pot on high heat until jam like, around 5 min
4. Top blueberries on the cheesecake when ready to serve
