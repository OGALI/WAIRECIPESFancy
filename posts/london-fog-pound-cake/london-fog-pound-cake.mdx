<StyledParagraph>Enjoy this healthy cake with a morning tea, coffee or whatever gets you started! Moist cake with the perfect crumble that will leave you feeling guilt free!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 15 minutes | 1 hour |  6  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>2 cups AP flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2/3 cup sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>tea leaves from 4 tea bags (2 Tbsp)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 flax egg (1Tbsp ground flaxseed and 3Tbsp water)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/3 cup vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/3 cup soy yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2/3 cup plant based milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp vanilla extract</ListItem>

## Steps

1. Mix all wet ingredients together and mix all dry ingredients in a seperate bowl
2. Poor dry ingredients into wet and stir until well incorporated
3. Bake in a standard bread pan at 350 deg F for 50-55min until toothpick inserted comes out clean
