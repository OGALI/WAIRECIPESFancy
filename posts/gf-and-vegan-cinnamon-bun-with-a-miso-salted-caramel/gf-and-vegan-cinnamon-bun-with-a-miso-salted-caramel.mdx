<StyledParagraph>These cinnamon rolls are legitametely the BEST. Super moist and full of cinnamon. Topped with a salted miso caramel which makes this breakfast/dessert sweet and salty. # Ingredients

## Cinnamon Bun
- <ListItem>2 cups GF oat flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 banana, mashed</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp maple syrup</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>pinch of salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp ground cinnamon</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp granulated sugar</ListItem>

## Vegan Miso Caramel 
- <ListItem>100g granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g vegan butter or margarine</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60ml cashew milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1.5 tbsp white miso</ListItem>

## Miso Caramel (non-vegan alternative)
- <ListItem>1/3 cup granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup Heavy Cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp white miso</ListItem>

# Steps

1. Mix all the ingredients together except sugar and cinnamon. Roll out dough into a 1/4 inch sheet
2. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Cut the rolled out dough through the center and roll to form the bun 
3. Bake at 350 deg F for 10-15min 
4. Vegan caramel: Heat sugar in saucepan. Once the sugar has melted, stir in the vegan butter and Whisk in the cashew milk. Remove the pan from the heat, and whisk in the miso.
5. Non-vegan caramel: Heat water and sugar in a saucepan. When sugar becomes a deep golden brown remove pan from heat. Stir in cream and miso
6. Spread caramel over the buns and enjoy warm!
