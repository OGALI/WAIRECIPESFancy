<StyledParagraph>Simple breakfast that anyone can make. Delicious vibrant matcha tea packed with antioxidants topped with a black sesame butter will sure get your mornings going!</StyledParagraph>

| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1 cup plant based milk of your choice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup quick oats</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp matcha tea</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp chia seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp maple syrup</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Mix all ingredients together. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight.
2. Add toppings of your choice: banana, raspberries, black sesame butter, cereal topping (chia, hemp, raw pumpkin seeds, raw buckwheat, coconut flakes)
