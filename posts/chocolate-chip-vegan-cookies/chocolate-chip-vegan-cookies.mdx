<StyledParagraph>Super moist chocolate chip cookies made from chickpeas! Vegan and gluten free that will sure make you feel guilt free after consuming a dozen at a time. Recipe adapted from <a href="https://detoxinista.com/vegan-chickpea-chocolate-chip-cookies-gluten-free-nut-free/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Detoxinista</a></StyledParagraph>

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1 1/2 cups cooked chickpeas , or 1 (15 oz.) can, drained and rinsed</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup gluten-free oat flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3-4 medjol dates</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tablespoons melted coconut oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tablespoon vanilla extract</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 teaspoon salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 teaspoon baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 teaspoon raw apple cider vinegar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3/4 cup dark chocolate chips</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Blend the chickpeas, melted coconut oil, dates, vanilla and apple cider vinegar until smooth. 
2. Mix in the oat flour, salt, chocolate chips and baking soda. 
3. Form into a cookie shape and bake at 350F for 8-10 mins.
