<StyledParagraph>Perfect cold noodle dish to enjoy for the first day of spring🌷🌷🌷</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 10 min | 10 min |  2   | Easy  |

# Ingredients
- <ListItem>2 bundles soba (6 oz)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 daikon, grated and liquid squeezed out</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 handful dried bonito flakes</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 green onion, chopped</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 red radish, sliced thin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>white sesame seeds, roasted</ListItem>

## Sauce
- <ListItem>1 cup dashi</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp mirin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tbsp soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>salt and pepper, to taste</ListItem>

# Preparation

1. Cook soba in boiling water until desired texture is achieved (6-7 min)
2. Combine sauce ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil 
3. Cool sauce in an ice bath. Poor over noodles and add the toppings (daikon, green onions, radish, sesame seeds, bonito flakes)
