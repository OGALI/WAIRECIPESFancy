<StyledParagraph>Korean rice cakes in a spicy and sweet sauce. These rice cakes are soft, chewy and savoury which makes for a great lunch or even snack! Recipe adapted from <a href="https://mykoreankitchen.com/tteokbokki-spicy-rice-cakes/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">mykoreankitchen</a></StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 15 min | 20 min |  4  | Easy  |

# Ingredients

- <ListItem>350g Korean rice cakes</ListItem>
- <ListItem>150g Korean fish cakes, cut into bite size pieces</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 cups Dashi (Add dried kelp to water abs let boil. Remove kelp afterwards.)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g white onion, thinly sliced</ListItem>

## Sauce
- <ListItem>3 Tbsp gochujang</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 1/2 Tbsp raw sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp minced garlic</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)</ListItem>

# Steps

1. Add Korean rice cakes, fish cakes, and onions to boiling dashi. Mix sauce ingredients and add to broth. 
2. Let cook on medium until broth thickens (15-20 minutes)
3. Top with sesame seeds, green onions and a hard boiled egg
