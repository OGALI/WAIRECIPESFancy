| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 1min | 5min |  2 cups  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>4 tbsp cacao powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>a pich of salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>500ml milk</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Disolve cacoa powder and sugar in a pan with the milk on medium heat for about 5 minutes.

2. Add a pinch of salt, wisk well and pour into heat proof mugs.

3. Top with whipped cream or marshmallows.
