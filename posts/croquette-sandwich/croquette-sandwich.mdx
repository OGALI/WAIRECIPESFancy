# Ingredients

## Potato mixture
- <ListItem>3 russet potatoes</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 white onion</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup corn</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 peas</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 carrots, cubed</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp curry powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 cube Japanese curry roux</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Salt and pepper to taste</ListItem>

## Coating
- <ListItem>2 cups panko</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup AP flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 eggs</ListItem>

## Sandwich components
- <ListItem>white bread</ListItem>
- <ListItem>lettuce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>tomato</ListItem>
- <ListItem>red cabbage</ListItem>
- <ListItem>mayonnaise</ListItem>
- <ListItem>tonkatsu sauce</ListItem>

# Steps
1. Cut the potatoes into 1/16 size pieces and boil in water until cooked (fork inserted pierces through easily). Drain and make sure water is evaporated.
2. Chop onions and cook in a pan with the oil until translucent. Add in the curry roux dissolved with the water. Mix until well incorporated.
3. Mash the potatoes in a bowl, but keep it chunky. Mix in the rest of the vegetables, curry powder, onion mixture, salt and pepper to taste.
4. Form 1 inch thick square patties to fit the bread or shape them as you like. Make sure that it is compact and the air is mostly out.
5. Coat potato patty into flour, then egg followed by panko.
6. Fry in vegetable oil at 350 deg F until golden brown (around 2-3 minutes)
*** If you want to bake or air fry, preheat oven to 400 deg F and toast the panko in advance
7. Spread mayo on both sides of the bread and drizzle tonkatsu sauce over one side of the croquette. Place cabbage then croquette then tomato then lettuce on bread and close up with the other.
8. ENJOY THIS THICK SANDWICH.
