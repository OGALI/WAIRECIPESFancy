# Ingredients

- <ListItem>1 cup quinoa</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 cups water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 chicken breast</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp avocado oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp ground cumin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 garlic cloves, minced </ListItem> 
## Tomato Salsa
- <ListItem>1 cup of tomato</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 red onion, minced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup cilantro, chopped</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 lemon, juiced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Salt and pepper to taste</ListItem>
## Corn Salsa
- <ListItem>1 cup of corn</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 red onion, minced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup cilantro, chopped</ListItem>

# Preparation

1. Incorporate the water and quinoa. Boil on med-high heat, once boiling turn down the heat and simmer for 20min 
2. Marinate chicken breast in oil, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper for 30min. Bake at 425 deg F for 15-18 min (internal temperature should be 165 deg F)
