<StyledParagraph>Fresh, light and delicate cheesecake with a strawberry jelly filling. This cheesecake is a perfect go-to summer recipe and is very simple to make.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hours | 25 min |  6  | Advanced  |

# Ingredients

## G√©noise 
- <ListItem>3 eggs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>75 g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>8 g honey</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 g vanilla extract</ListItem>
- <ListItem>A few drops of red food dye</ListItem>
- <ListItem>25 g unsalted butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40 g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>90 g cake flour</ListItem>
## Strawberry Jelly 
- <ListItem>80 g warm water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20 g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>130 g chopped strawberries</ListItem>
## Cream Cheese Filling 
- <ListItem>230 g cream cheese</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60 g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>50 g yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 g vanilla extract</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5 g lemon juice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5 g gelatin</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>20 g warm water</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>100 g whipped cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Sliced strawberries</ListItem> 


# Steps 
1. For the g√©noise: continuously lightly stir eggs, sugar and honey over a double boiler until warm. 
2. Once warm, whip mixture in a stand mixture until thick and pale in colour. Add vanilla and red food dye and then mix in flour. 
3. Melt butter with milk in a separate bowl. Add around a 1/4 cup of the cake batter to the milk-butter mixture and incorporate. 
4. Transfer back to the cake batter and mix until incorporated. 
5. Pour batter into a 7 inch cake pan and bake at 350 F for 20-25 mins. 
6. Cool the cake down and cut away the top and bottom layers. Cut cake into 1 cm and 1.5 cm thick pieces and place it onto a sheet. Cut cake pieces into 6 inch diameter pieces. 
7. For the strawberry jelly, dissolve sugar and gelatin in the warm water. Incorporate chopped strawberries. 
8. Pour into a 5 inch mold and let set in the fridge until firm (30mins).
9. For the cream cheese filling, cream the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add yogurt, vanilla and lemon juice. Dissolve gelatin in warm water and add to filling. 
10. In a separate bowl, beat cream until stiff peaks. Fold cream into filling. 
11. In a 6 inch springform pan, place the 1.5 cm thick cake piece. Place sliced strawberries around the pan and then pour half of the cream cheese filling mix into the pan. 
12. Place strawberry jelly on top of the cream cheese layer and cover with remaining filling. 
13. Place top cake layer. Let the cake set for 3 hours in the fridge before serving.
