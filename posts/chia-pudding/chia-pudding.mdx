<StyledParagraph>Great recipe to switch up your everyday breakfast foods. This will definitely feel like you are eating dessert for breakfast! The combination of the sweet and tangy cherries with rich and decadent chocolate go hand in hand!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 10 min | 0 min |  2  | Easy  |

# Ingredients

## Chocolate Pudding
- <ListItem>3tbsp chia</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp cacao powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3/4 cup plant based milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp maple syrup</ListItem>
## Cherry Jam
- <ListItem>1 cup cherries, pitted</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp chia seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp lemon juice</ListItem>
## Topping
- <ListItem>cocoa nibs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>greek yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>dark chocolate, shaved</ListItem>

# Steps 
1. Mix chia seeds with cocoa powder and break up clumps. Add milk and maple syrup. Stir until combined and let sit for at least 2 hours.
2. For the jam, cook the cherries in a saucepan until bubbling and syrupy. Put cooked cherries in a blender and add the rest of the ingredients. Pulse in blender until desired texture.
