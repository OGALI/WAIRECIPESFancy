# Ingredients

## Filling
- <ListItem>400g minced pork or meat of your choice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>80ml soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>80ml sesame oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40ml oyster sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>4 tbsp corn starch</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 whole cabbage</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Lots of chives</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tablespoon of garlic</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tablespoon of ginger</ListItem>

## Dough
- <ListItem>400g AP flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>200ml hot water, add 2-3 tbsp at a time as needed</ListItem>
- <ListItem>salt</ListItem>

# Steps

1. To make dough, mix all ingredients by kneading and let rest for 10-20mins
2. Mix the meat with the soy sauce, sesame oil, oyster sauce and corn starch. Add in the chopped vegetables and aromatics. 
3. Form dumplings, should make ~ 100 pieces 
4. Heat up the pan with little oil and lay gyoza. Put water just below the gyoza and cover the lid. Cook for about 5 mins until water has evaporated
5. Open the lid and add some sesame oil, immediately cover back with the lid and let it cook for another 2 mins until golden brown
