<StyledParagraph>This light cheesecake is packed with matcha flavour! Made this cheesecake again but instead of 2 layers, there’s 3 layers!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hours | 7 min |  6  | Advanced  |

# Ingredients

## Crust
- <ListItem>45g graham cracker crumbs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>23 g melted butter</ListItem>

## Cream cheese layer
- <ListItem>300g cream cheese</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>7.5g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>45ml water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>150ml heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>10g sifted baking grade matcha powder «Kokura»</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60ml milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>7.5ml lemon juice</ListItem>

## Matcha jelly
- <ListItem>1.5 sifted baking grade matcha powder «Kokura»</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>18g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>53ml water</ListItem>

# Steps 
1. For the crust, mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press onto bottom of 6inch spring form pan.
2. Bake in the over at 350F for 7 mins. Let cool.
3. For the cheese layer, cream the cream cheese with sugar until smooth. Mix water and gelatin in a separate bowl and heat in microwave for 10secs. Add to the cream cheese mix. 
4. In a separate bowl, whip cream until soft peaks. Fold cream into cream cheese mix. 
5. Separate the cream cheese mix into 3 (1/3 for the white layer and 2/3 for the matcha layer). In a separate bowl, dissolve matcha in milk. Add to the cream cheese mix with 2/3 of the mixture. Add lemon juice to the other cream cheese mix.
6. Pour half of the matcha cream cheese mix into the pan. Refrigerate for 30 mins. 
7. In the meantime, prepare the matcha jelly layer. Mix all ingredients together and heat in microwave for 10secs. 
8. After 15 mins have passed, pour the second cream cheese mix on top of the matcha layer. Wait another 15min and pour the remaining matcha mixture into the pan.
9. Pour matcha jelly mix over second layer.
10. Let refrigerate for a few hours until set and serve.
