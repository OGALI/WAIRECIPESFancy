# Ingredients 
- <ListItem>8 strawberries (plus additional for ganish)</ListItem>
## Cake
- <ListItem>150g egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>85g granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>6g honey</ListItem>
- <ListItem>60g cake flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>10g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>10g butter</ListItem>

## Cream
- <ListItem>350g heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g granulated sugar</ListItem>

# Steps

1. In a large bowl, combine the eggs, sugar and honey. Place bowl over a double boiler and whisk constantly until mixture reaches 100-110 deg F
2. Remove bowl from heat and whisk egg mixture until pale and volume has increased x3 (5-7 min)
3. In a seperate bowl, melt butter and milk together 
4. Sift cake flour into the egg mixture and fold until well incorporated 
5. Mix 1 cup of the batter into the butter and milk mixture 
6. Poor the mixture in step 5 back into the large bowl with the rest of the batter. Fold until well incorporated
7. Bake at 350 deg F for 15-20 min in a 28cmx28cm square pan
8. Prepare the cream and slice straberries into 1/6 size pieces 
9. Cut the cake into 6.5cm wide strips (4 equal strips)
10. Spread cream and strawberries on the cake evenly 
11. Roll the first strip and place vertically on a plate. Roll the next strips around the first and next strips
12. Spread the remaining icing on the outside of the cake. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours before serving
