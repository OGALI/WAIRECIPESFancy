<StyledParagraph>Spicy, but refreshing Korean dish packed with crunchy vegetables and chewy cold buckwheat noodles. This can be enjoyed as a main meal or as a side dish to a bbq. Tastes super delicious when wrapped in a perilla leaf, that is our personal favorite!</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 20 min | 10 min |  2   | Easy  |

# Ingredients
- <ListItem>2 bundles soba noodle</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tomato</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 lettuce bundle</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 cup red cabbage</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 bell pepper</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>2 eggs, hard boiled</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>sesame seeds</ListItem> 

## Sauce
- <ListItem>1.5 tbsp gochujang (korean chili paste)</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp granulated sugar</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1/2 tbsp gochugaru (korean chilli pepper flakes)</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp rice wine vinegar</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp mirin</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp soy sauce</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp sesame oil</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 tbsp sesame seed</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>2 garlic cloves, grated</ListItem> 

# Steps
1. Cook noodles in boiling water for 5-7 minutes or per package instructions
2. Drain and rince noodles in cold water
3. Mix all sauce ingredients together in a bowl
4. Mix noodles with sauce and add in the prepared vegetables
5. Serve and enjoy (●'◡'●)
