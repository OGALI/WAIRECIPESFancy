## Ingredients

- <ListItem>2 cups sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3/4 cup dutch process cocoa powder, plus more for dusting</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 teaspoon salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 teaspoon baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 teaspoons baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 cup sour milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 cup freshly brewed strong black coffee</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 eggs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 teaspoon vanilla</ListItem>

## Cream
- <ListItem>1 cup heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp granulated sugar</ListItem>

## Blood
- <ListItem>2 cups fresh or frozen raspberries</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup granulated sugar</ListItem>

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F. Line muffin pan with muffin tins. 
2. Sift sugar, flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder and baking soda in a bowl. Set aside.
3. In a seperate bowl, add the milk, coffee, vegetable oil, eggs and vanilla one at a time. Mix until everything is incorporated. 
4. Slowly add in the dry ingredients. Once all of the flour mixture is added, mix the batter until there are no lumps.
5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 20 minutes, or until a cake tester comes out clean. Allow to cool to room temperature on a wire rack. 
6. For the cream, whip cream with a whisk until soft peaks. Gradually poor in sugar and incorporate until dissolved. 
7. For the blood, press raspberries through a sieve to remove the seeds. Mix in the sugar. 
8. Pipe the cream onto the cupcakes once they are cooled. Stain the blood on the cupcakes, get creative!
