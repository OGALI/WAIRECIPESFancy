<StyledParagraph>Sweet purple potato swirled in a fluffy and pillowy milk bread dough. Get those carbs in and your veggies all together!</StyledParagraph>

## Ingredients
- <ListItem>600g bread flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>12g milk powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>42g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>6g yeast</ListItem>
- <ListItem>7g salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>150g water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>300g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g unsalted butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g mashed purple sweet</ListItem>

## Steps

1. To activate the yeast, mix water, milk and sugar in a large bowl. Heat in the microwave until warm. Add the yeast and let sit for 5min.
2. Add salt and the milk powder to the yeast mixture and stir 
3. Add the bread flour and knead by hand or in a stand mixer with a bread attachment for 15 minutes. Once the mixture starts to come together, fold in the butter
4. Once the dough is smooth, seperate the dough in two equal parts. Knead in the sweet potato in one of the halves until well incorporated
5. Place the dough in two seperate bowls and let proof until doubled in size (1.5-2h)
6. Roll dough seperately with a rolling pin into equal rectangle sizes that are 0.5 inch thick. Place sweet potato dough onto the top of the plain dough
7. Roll the dough into a log and pinch the ends tightly. Place into a bread pan (13x5x5.5). Let proof for another 45 minutes to 1 hour 
8. Bake in the oven at 350 deg F for 40 minutes
