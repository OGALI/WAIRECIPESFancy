<StyledParagraph>Time to change it up for breakfast and have a pizza instead. A fun guilt free nutritious meal to enjoy and customize as you please.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 min | 12 min |  1  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1/4 cup oats</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup oat flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 tsp cinnamon</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp mashed banana</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 flax egg (1 Tbsp flaxseed ground +3 Tbsp water)</ListItem>

## Steps 
1. Mix all wet ingredients and then stir in the dry ingredients. 
2. Form a pizza crust and bake in the oven at 350 deg F for 10-12 min.
3. Garnish the pizza with the toppings of your choice (maple syrup, nut butter, fruit of your choice)
