<StyledParagraph>Airy, light and chewy all in one bite, this doughnut recipe is the perfect staple! The floral and sweet honey balanced with salt and warm spices, is a must try combination. You can alternatively air-fry the doughnuts and they turn out just as good. # Ingredients

## Dough 
- <ListItem>117g sticky rice flour (glutinous rice flour or mochiko flour)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>235g boiling water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>200g bread flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>190g all-purpose flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 large egg, plus 1 yolk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>46g) granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 1/2 teaspoons instant dry yeast</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp sea salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>13g unsalted butter</ListItem>

## Honey Glaze
- <ListItem>1/4 cup honey</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tbsp unsalted butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp sea salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp ground green cardamom</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp ground cinnamon</ListItem>

# Steps 

1. In a stand mixer fitted with the dough attachement, add the rice flour and boiling water and mix until the batter becomes sticky 
2. Add the bread flour, AP flour, eggs, sugar, yeast and salt 
3. Knead on low speed until mixture comes together
4. Add the butter and continue to knead for 8 min until the dough is smooth and elastic
5. Let the dough rise at room temperature until doubled, around 2h
6. Roll out the dough until 1cm thick and cut the donuts with a 4-inch cutter
7. Allow the dough to rise for another 1.5-2h 
8. To make the glaze, combine the honey, butter and salt in a pot and cook over low heat until the mixture has come to a simmer. Let cool to room temperature and mix in the spices
9. Fry the donuts at 300 deg F and flip every 1min 
10. To serve, drizzle the glaze over the donuts and enjoy warm!
