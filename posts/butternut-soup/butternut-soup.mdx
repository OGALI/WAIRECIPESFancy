## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1 butternut pumpkin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 stick of celery</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 carrots</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp white miso paste disolved in 1 litre of water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp crushed/grated fresh ginger</ListItem>
- <ListItem>a pinch of chilli flakes</ListItem>
- <ListItem>fresh or dried coriander to garnish</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Simmer the butternut, cellery, carrots, ginger, chilli flakes in a large pot along with the miso stock for about 30 minutes on medium heat.

2. Once soft, let it cool and puree the soup with a blender.

3. Once pureed, heat up the soup before serving.

4. Garnish with coriander an chilli flakes.
