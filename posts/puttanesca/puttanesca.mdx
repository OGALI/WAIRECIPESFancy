<StyledParagraph>If you want to try something new and are tired of making the same noodle dish every night, try out this super fresh and simple pasta recipe that you can whip up in no time! This sweet tomato based sauce is balanced out with briny capers and olives and finally topped off with bright herbs. </StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 30 min | 15 min | 4 | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1lb red tomatoes, seeds removed</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp red pepper chili flakes</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp. kosher salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 garlic cloves, grated</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup parsley, chopped fine</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1¬Ω cup cherry tomatoes, halved</ListItem> 
- <ListItem>1 cup green olives, sliced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp drained capers, chopped</ListItem>
- <ListItem>360g dried spaghetti</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp unsalted butter</ListItem>


## Steps
1. Pulse tomatoes, red pepper flakes, and salt in a food processor until smooth
2. Transfer sauce to a large bowl and mix in garlic, oil, parsley, cherry tomatoes, olives and capers
3. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente
4. Drain pasta and stir in butter
5. Mix pasta into bowl with tomato mixture. Toss vigorously with tongs and serve!
