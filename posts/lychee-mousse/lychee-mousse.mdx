<StyledParagraph>Fresh and fruity mousse cake that is both sweet and tart. The floral flavour from the lychee is what makes this mousse cake so addictive.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hours | 20 min |  6  | Advanced  |

# Ingredients

## Génoise 
- <ListItem>75 egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g granulated sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3g honey</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g cake flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5g butter</ListItem>

## Raspberry Jelly
- <ListItem>120g raspberry purée</ListItem>
- <ListItem>10g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>10 ml water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp lemon juice</ListItem>

## Lychee mousse  
- <ListItem>3g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>15ml hot water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g lychee purée</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g whipped cream + 7 g sugar</ListItem>

## Raspberry mousse 
- <ListItem>3g gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>15ml hot water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g raspberry purée</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp lemon juice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g whipped cream + 7 g sugar</ListItem>

## Raspberry glaze 
- <ListItem>20g hot water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2g sheet gelatin</ListItem>
- <ListItem>50g raspberry purée</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp lemon juice</ListItem>

# Steps
1. For the cake: In a large bowl, combine the eggs, sugar and honey. Place bowl over a double boiler and whisk constantly until mixture reaches 100-110 deg F
2. Remove bowl from heat and whisk egg mixture until pale and volume has increased x3 (5-7 min)
3. In a seperate bowl, melt butter and milk together
4. Sift cake flour into the egg mixture and fold until well incorporated
5. Mix 1/2 cup of the batter into the butter and milk mixture
6. Poor the mixture in step 5 back into the large bowl with the rest of the batter. Fold until well incorporated
7. Bake at 350 deg F for 15-20 min in a 9x13inch pan
8. Once cooked, cut out a 6inch cake circle 
9. To make the raspberry jelly, dissolve the gelatin in water. Add dissolved gelatin to puréed raspberry, lemon and sugar. 
10. Microwave mixture for 20 secs until gelatin is completely dissolved
11. Line a 5inch cake mold with plastic wrap and pour raspberry jelly mixture
12. Let set in fridge for and hour or freeze
13. To make the lychee mouse, combine water and gelatin until dissolved
14. Add gelatin mix to the puréed lychee.
15. Microwave mixture for 20 secs until gelatin is completely dissolved.
16. Whip 200g of whip cream until stiff peaks with 14g of sugar.
17. Fold 100g of the whip cream mixture to the lychee mixture 
18. To make the raspberry mousse, combine water and gelatin until dissolved.
19. Add gelatin mix and lemon juice to the puréed raspberry 
20. Fold in the rest of the cream
21. To assemble, place the cake layer followed by the lychee mousse. Take the raspberry jelly out of the mold and place on top of lychee mousse layer. Add raspberry mouse. 
22. Let the cake chill for 30 mins before pouring of the raspberry glaze
23. To make the raspberry glaze, combine all ingredients
24. Pour raspberry glaze SLOWLY on top of the cake.
25. Let chill for 3 hrs and serve!
