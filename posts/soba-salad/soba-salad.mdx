<StyledParagraph>Super refreshing dish to enjoy in the summer! The dressing for this dish is inspired by the one used for Hiyashi Chuka, a Japanese cold ramen dish.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 20 min | 10 min |  2   | Easy  |

# Ingredients
- <ListItem>2 bundles soba (6 oz)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cucumber</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tomato</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 cups romain lettuce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 hard boiled eggs</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 green onion, chopped</ListItem>
- <ListItem>white & black sesame seeds, roasted</ListItem>

## Sauce
- <ListItem>3 Tbsp soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1.5 Tbsp rice vinegar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp sesame oil (roasted)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp water</ListItem>

# Preparation

1. Cook soba in boiling water until desired texture is achieved (6-7 min)
2. Combine sauce ingredients in a bowl 
3. Rince noodles in cold water. Poor dressing over noodles. Add all the vegetables and garnish
