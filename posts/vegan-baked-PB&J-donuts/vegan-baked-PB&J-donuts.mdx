<StyledParagraph>Healthy breakfast that will get you confused if it should be a dessert instead 😋. Peanut butter donut doused with a chocolate glaze, drizzled with peanut butter, dolloped with a raspberry compote and sprinkled with some homemade granola.</StyledParagraph>

# Ingredients
## Peanut Butter Donut
- <ListItem>1 cup (125 g) Unbleached All-Purpose Flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3/4 tsp baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 tsp baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 tsp salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 flax eggs (2 tbsp ground flax+ 6 tbsp water)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/3 cup (72 g) brown sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup (60 ml) non-dairy milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup (2 oz) soy yogurt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tbsp (32 g) mmooth peanut butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tbsp (45 ml) avocado/vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp vanilla extract</ListItem> 

## Chocolate Ganache
- <ListItem>2 oz semi sweet chocolate, melted</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/4 cup full fat coconut milk (scrape the top of can)</ListItem>

## Peanut Butter Drizzle
- <ListItem>2 tbsp peanut butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Oil if necessary</ListItem>

## Raspberry Compote
- <ListItem>1/2 cup raspberry</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tbsp sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tbsp lemon juice</ListItem>

## Homemade Granola
Recipe by Cookie and Kate, for the full recipe click <a href="https://cookieandkate.com/healthy-granola-recipe/" target="_blank"  rel="noreferrer noopener">here</a> 

# Steps 

1. For the batter, mix wet ingredients and dry ingredients separately. Add dry to wet ingredients and mix.
2. Fill the batter in a donut pan with 12 molds and bake in the oven at 350 deg F for 20 min until toothpick inserted comes out clean
3. For the ganache, heat the chocolate and coconut milk on a double boiler until melted 
4. For the raspberry compote, heat raspberries and sugar on med-high heat until the mixture thickens and ressembles a jam. Stir in lemon juice once done cooking
5. To assemble, dip donuts first in the ganache. Spoon the raspberry in the center and drizzle with the peanut butter. Sprinkle granola and hemp if you like
