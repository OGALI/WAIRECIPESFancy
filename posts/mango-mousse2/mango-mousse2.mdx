# Steps
1. In a large bowl, combine the eggs, sugar and honey. Place bowl over a double boiler and whisk constantly until mixture reaches 100-110 deg F
2. Remove bowl from heat and whisk egg mixture until pale and volume has increased x3 (5-7 min)
3. In a seperate bowl, melt butter and milk together
4. Sift cake flour into the egg mixture and fold until well incorporated
5. Mix 1/2 cup of the batter into the butter and milk mixture
6. Poor the mixture in step 5 back into the large bowl with the rest of the batter. Fold until well incorporated
7. Bake at 350 deg F for 15-20 min in a 9x13inch pan
8. Once cooked, cut out a 6inch cake circle 
9. To make the mango jelly, combine all ingredients and place in a 5 inch mold lined with plastic wrap. Let set in fridge for and hour.
10. In a bowl combine sugar and egg yolk. 
11. Heat milk until warm and add to the egg yolk mixture. Return the mix back to the stove and heat up while constantly stirring until mixture has thickened. 
12. Add gelatin and white chocolate 
13. Whip 200g of whip cream until stiff peaks with 14g of sugar. 
14. Fold in half of the whip cream into the white chocolate mixture.
15. To make the mango mousse, combine water and gelatin until dissolved.
16. Add gelatin mix and lemon juice to the pur√©ed mango.
17. Fold in the rest of the cream
18. To assemble, place the cake layer followed by the white chocolate mousse in a 6 inch springform pan. Take the mango jelly out of the mold and place on top of white chocolate mousse layer. Add mango mousse over the jelly.
19. To make the mango glaze, combine all ingredients.
20. Pour mango glaze on top of the cake.
21. Let chill for 3 hrs and serve!
