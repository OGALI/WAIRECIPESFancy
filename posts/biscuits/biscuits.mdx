<StyledParagraph>Pillowy and savoury cheesy garlic bread that is the perfect accompaniment to a delicious meal! This is a simple recipe that you can whip up in one bowl.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 10 min | 15-20 min |  8-10 biscuits  | Easy  |

## Ingredients

- <ListItem>1/2 cup butter, unsalted</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 cups AP flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp sea salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 garlic cloves, grated</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2/3 cup cheddar cheese, grated</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 cup buttermilk (or milk + 1tbsp vinegar)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Dried or fresh herbs to top (optional)</ListItem>

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 400 deg F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
2. Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl (flour, baking powder + soda, salt)
3. Measure cold butter and crumble it into the flour mixture with a pastry cutter/fork or hands until the butter ressembles small peas
4. Stir the grated cheese and garlic into the bowl with the flour mixture 
5. Pour in the buttermilk and stir with a spoon until well incorporated 
6. Drop large spoonfuls of batter onto the baking tray (2-3 tbsp), spacing them at least 1 inch apart and bake for 15-20 minutes
7. Once the biscuits are done baking, brush them with some garlic butter and top with herbs (optional if you want to save the calories ðŸ˜‰)
8. ENJOY \^o^/
