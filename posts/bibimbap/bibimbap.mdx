<StyledParagraph>Flavourful, spicy and crispy rice bowl that is super simple to make for a lunch or dinner.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 15 min | 10 min |  4   | Easy  |

# Ingredients

- <ListItem>250g seasoned spinach (cook in garlic and sesame oil)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>350g mildly seasoned bean sprouts (boil and mix with sesame oil, sugar and soy sauce)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g (3.5 ounces) shiitake mushroom</ListItem>
- <ListItem>120g (4.2 ounces) carrots (1 small)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp fine sea salt (1/4 tsp each will be used when cooking shiitake mushroom and carrots)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 to 4 serving portions of steamed rice</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 or 4 eggs (depending on the serving portion)</ListItem>

## Bulgogi
- <ListItem>800g rib eye cut in thin pieces</ListItem>
- <ListItem>6 Tbsp soy sauce</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 Tbsp brown sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp rice wine (mirin)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 asian pear or red apple (155 g / 5.5 ounces)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 onion (80 g / 2.8 ounces)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp minced garlic</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp minced ginger</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/8 tsp ground black pepper</ListItem>

## Bibimbap Sauce
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp gochujang</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp sesame oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp water</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Tbsp roasted sesame seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp apple vinegar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp minced garlic</ListItem>

# Preparation

1. Mix rib-eye with marinade ingredients and let sit for at least 3h
2. Cook bulgogi on the grill or in a cast iron pan. Let cooked rice crisp up in pan with sesame oil
3. Mix all the sauce ingredients together until well incorporated
4. Cook egg and top with vegetables
5. Mix with bibimbap sauce
