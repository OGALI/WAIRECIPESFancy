# Ingredients

## Falafel
- <ListItem>1 can chickpeas drained</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/3 cup cilantro</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 garlic cloves minced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 red onion</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 Tbsp sesame seeds</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tsp cumin ground</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3-4 Tbsp oat flour (if needed)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>salt and pepper to taste</ListItem>
## Topping 
- <ListItem>cucumber</ListItem>
- <ListItem>tomato</ListItem>
- <ListItem>red onion, sliced</ListItem>
- <ListItem>vegan feta</ListItem>
- <ListItem>dressing for vegetables (olive oil, lemon juice, salt & pepper to taste)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>sauce (soy yogurt, cucumber diced, dried oregano and lemon juice)</ListItem>

# Steps 

1. Blend all ingredients together except flour
2. Add flour to mixture at the end if the consistency is too wet
3. Form falafels and bake at 400 degrees for 10 min or pan fry until golden brown
4. Assemble the sandwiches with pita or flatbread of your choice
