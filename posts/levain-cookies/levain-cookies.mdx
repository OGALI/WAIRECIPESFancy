# Ingredients

## Matcha Cookie
- <ListItem>95g Cold Butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>65g Caster Sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>65g Light Brown Sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g White Chocolate</ListItem>
- <ListItem>210g Plain Flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>Heaped tbsp Matcha Powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5g Baking Powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp Salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Egg</ListItem>

## Black Sesame Cookie
- <ListItem>50g Cold Butter</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g Caster Sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g White Chocolate</ListItem>
- <ListItem>100g semi sweet chocolate</ListItem>
- <ListItem>200g Plain Flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3g Baking Powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1g baking soda</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp Salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 Egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>25g cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>150g black sesame paste</ListItem>
- <ListItem>5g vanilla extract</ListItem>

# Steps

1. For the matcha cookie, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
2. Add egg and mix until incorporated. Mix dry ingredients and add to wet ingredients. Add chocolate chips
3. For the black sesame cookie, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
4. Add egg and black sesame paste. Mix until incorporated. Mix dry ingredients and add to wet ingredients. Add chocolate chips
5. Take a tablespoon full of the matcha batter and a tablespoon full of the black sesame batter. Bring together in a ball
6. Flatten slightly onto the baking sheet and bake at 350F for 12 mins. This recipe makes around 12 cookies.
