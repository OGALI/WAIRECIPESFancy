<StyledParagraph>Delicious tea infused flavored cake that will brighten your afternoon. Try this cake out with any of your favorite loose teas.</StyledParagraph>

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 20 min | 30 min |  6  | Intermediate  |

# Ingredients 
## Cake
- <ListItem>45g egg yolk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2g vanilla Extract</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20g brown sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1g salt</ListItem>
- <ListItem>28g vegetable oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g earl grey milk (steep 4g of early grey in 60g of hot milk for 10min)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>55g cake flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2g baking powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2g loose earl grey tea (1 tea bag)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>90g egg white</ListItem>
- <ListItem>45g granulated sugar</ListItem>
## Cream 
- <ListItem>250g heavy cream</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g granulated sugar</ListItem>

# Steps

1. In a large bowl, whisk egg yolks, vanilla and brown sugar until mixture turns pale (5 min)
2. In a seperate bowl, sift dry ingredients together (flour, baking powder, salt, early grey) and mix
3. Add vegetable oil and earl grey milk into egg yolk mixture and stir. Mix in the dry ingredients. 
4. In another bowl, whisk egg whites until frothy and gradually add in the granulated sugar until stiff peaks
5. Fold egg whites into batter in 2 additions and transfer into a 6 inch chiffon pan 
6. Bake in the oven at 350 deg F for around 30 min. Once out of the oven, cool the cake inverted
