# Steps

1. To make pickled vegetables: Mix 2 Tbsp white sugar, 3 Tbsp rice wine vinegar, 2 Tbsp boiling water. Let vegetables sit in the pickle mixture for at least 20min
2. For tofu: combine all ingredients together and let tofu marinade for at least 30 minutes. Fry tofu on med-high heat with avocado oil
3. Mix all sauce ingredients together and plate with the rest of the ingredients
