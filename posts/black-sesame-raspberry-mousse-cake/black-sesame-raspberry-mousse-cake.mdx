<StyledParagraph>Deep roasted black sesame mousse paired with a fresh and tangy raspberry gelée are the perfect duo. This cake has the perfect balance between sweet and savoury notes. Recipe adapted from <a href="https://youtu.be/CUAPc1MzLcU">Sunday Baking</a></StyledParagraph>

| :--: | :--: | :------: | :---: |
| 5 hours | 20 min |  6  | Advanced  |

## Ingredients
# Raspberry Gelee
- <ListItem>230g fresh or frozen raspberries</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1.5 gelatin sheet</ListItem>
- <ListItem>20g sugar</ListItem>
- - <ListItem>1 tsp lemon juice</ListItem>
# Black Sesame Joconde 
- <ListItem>70g egg</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g black sesame paste</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g powdered sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g almond flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g egg white</ListItem>
- <ListItem>25g sugar</ListItem>
- <ListItem>30g AP flour</ListItem>
# Black Sesame Mousse 
- <ListItem>20g egg yolk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>70g milk</ListItem>
- <ListItem>40g black sesame paste</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 gelatin sheets</ListItem>
- <ListItem>130g white chocolate</ListItem>
- <ListItem>250 heavy cream</ListItem>

## Steps
1. To make raspeberry gelee, bloom gelatin sheet in cold water until soft (5 min)
2. Measure 230g of raspberries and push through a sieve to remove the seeds until you obtain 120g of raspberry puree. 
3. Place gelatin sheet in raspberry puree and microwave for 30s until gelatin sheet had melted 
4. Add sugar and lemon juice to mixture. Poor mixture into a 4.5-5 inch circular mold lined with plastic wrap. Place in the freezer until set or frozen. 
5. For the cake, whisk the egg, black sesame paste, powdered sugar and almond flour until the mixture turns paler (5min). Fold in flour into the mixture. 
6. Whisk egg whites in another bowl and gradually add in the sugar once you start to see soft peaks. Whisk until medium-stiff peaks 
7. Fold the egg whites into the black sesame mixture until incorporated
8. Bake in a a 9 inch cake pan at 350 deg celcius for 10-15min 
9. For the mousse, bloom gelatin sheets in cold water (5min) and transfer the sheets into a bowl with the white chocolate
10. Mix egg yolk, milk and black sesame in a small pot. Cook on med heat while constantly stirring until it thickens (you can see a trace when you scrape the pot) Poor this mixture over the white chocolate and bloomed gelatin. 
11. Whisk heavy cream until soft peaks and fold this into the other mixture once mixture is at room temperature. 
12. To assemble, cut cake into a 6 inch circle and place on the bottom of a 6 inch springfrom pan. Add half of the mousse mixture and set in the freezer for 10min.
13. Add raspberry disk on top of the mousse and cover with the remaining mousse. Let the cake set in the fridge for at least 5 hours. 
14. SLICE, SERVE AND ENJOY!
